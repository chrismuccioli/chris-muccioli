Project

Singles Club

Role

Creative Direction, Photography, Product Design, Print Design, Branding

Description

Singles Club was started in 2014 as a project between myself and writer/educator Jeffrey Silverstein. Looking to combine our strengths and release our friends music but not content with simply spinning up a record label we came up with the quarterly subscription club model.

year 2 records
year 2 records

Every quarter subscribers would receive a vinyl 45’ featuring an new original song by the featured artists, plus a audio interview on side b. Online at www.singlesclub.fm anyone can read the digital issue and listen to the single.
